360 Reframe

The objective of this project was to challenge our group to "disappear" out of the sight of the 360 camera. This forced us to play with the confines of stitch lines, edges, corpses, and the failure to disappear in a 360 space. Our group figured that the best way to approach this project was to attempt to capture a simple game of Hide and Seek, thus presenting you with the final product "Hide and Seek in Hell."

Role:

Camera Operator (Insta 360 X2),
Editing Assistant (Adobe PremierePro)
