American Dream Casino: HRO
(Final Showcase in 2026)

"American Dream Casino: HRO" (ADC) is an experiecne created by the artist, Robert Farid Karimi, Critically acclaimed trans-channel artist, interactive experience educator, performer, author, cultural activist Robert Farid Karimi, designs interactive immersive game-performance experiences to spark players to imagine worlds of mutual community nourishment. Of Iranian/Guatemalan/Mayan descent, Karimi's work frequently addresses themes of cultural heritage, social justice, and community engagement, bridging various artistic mediums to foster dialogue and human connection.

American Dream Casino is a playful, interactive, casino-like experience that celebrates what we gamble, and what we gain (and lose) to achieve the American Dream. Audience members become "high rollers" in a casino where they don't gamble with money, but with the values, freedoms, and sacrifices that constitute our collective understanding of the American Dream.

The show's inspiration comes from Karimi's past - their parents were gambling addicts and would spend hours gambling at casinos so that they could gain the American dream of prosperity. Unfortunately, this also meant they would leave Karimi in parking lots and casino lobbies for hours where they were left to fend for themself. American Dream Casino is their way to reconcile this quest of their parents with the abandonment they felt as a child.

At the core of this project lies the profound question: In a democratic society that promises opportunity for all, what are we truly willing to risk—and who actually bears those risks—in pursuit of their “American Dream”? The piece examines how democracy functions as both a system of governance and as an economic framework that shapes our opportunities, expectations, and outcomes. By placing participants in positions where they must literally gamble with their democratic values, the experience reveals the often invisible trade-offs embedded in the American system.

Following each performance, participants gather for a facilitated discussion where they share the choices they made, what they won or lost, and how these experiences reflect their actual relationships with each other, their families, and American democracy. These conversations create space for nuanced dialogue about representation, opportunity gaps, and civic responsibility that transcends typical political discourse.

This experience employs a dynamic, iterative development process that mirrors the democratic values it explores. Karimi used a devised practice centered around "playtesting," which allows them to refine the experience through active participation. This approach ensures the casino games not only function mechanically but effectively engage participants with the deeper questions about democracy at the heart of the project. As the core mechanics solidify, we expanded to public playtests with community members, gathering real-time feedback that shapes subsequent iterations. These sessions allowed us to calibrate the delicate balance between structured gameplay and spontaneous exchange that gives the piece its power. This methodology proved invaluable during our initial runs of Playtest 1 at La Jolla Playhouse (LJP) in San Diego. These experiences informed substantial revisions to both the world-building elements and the narrative design that guides participants through the casino. Playtest 2 in April 2025, built on insights from Playtest 1 to further develop the experience and test a custom-built fully functional slot machine.

In future playtests (3 and 4), in order to scale the performances for large audiences, Seema Sueko-Low will join as director, whose theatrical experience includes political, civil rights-themed performances and facilitation expertise in consensus organizing (see her work in Imagine a US without Racism). Future playtests will also test how American Dream Casino will serve as a platform for others to share their narratives. Aisa Villarosa will be the artist joining playtest 3 and 4 working to develop her personal narrative within the world of American Dream Casino.
