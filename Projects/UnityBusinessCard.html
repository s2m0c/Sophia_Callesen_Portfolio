AR Business Card

This project is still in the process of being finalized, as I am currently learning how to utilize and create different projects in Unity 3D. This AR Business card focuses on utilizing the Vuforia package to create a template that buttons and triggers can be added to within the engine as interactivity components. When held up to a camera within the software, the left "button" plays one video, while the right "button" plays another. *Template was created by Biayna Bogosian*
