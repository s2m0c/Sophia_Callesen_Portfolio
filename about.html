Sophia Callesen

Hi, I'm Sophie!

I'm an experience designer, world-builder, breaker, creator, artist, practitioner, and researcher interested in creating spaces of safety, inclusivity, and change through the power of immersive narrative.

My creative-process methodology and interests stem from utilizing my transdisciplinary background in the cognitive sciences and theatre to delve deeper into how to evoke certain emotions from participants on a physiological and behavioral level during an experience.

My work extends from immersive experience building, to game design, UI, performing, scientific-behavioral research studies, cultural sensitivity studies, web development, and graphic design.

I graduated from Arizona State University: Barrett, The Honors College with degrees in Biological Sciences (Neurobiology, Physiology, and Behavior), Theatre, and Italian. More recently, I've been teaching myself how to code in various languages in hopes that I will be able to create XR experiences through Unreal Engine and Unity.

My ultimate goal is to create XR (VR, AR, MR) immersive experiences that help build and provide a platform for minorities and those belonging to marginalized identities.

Personal Diversity Statement I Uphold in all of my Work & Practices:
~ I strive to foster a community that demands and creates positive change. People of all identities and expressions are welcome and heard. Welcome to our community; I can't wait to connect with you! ~


Please feel free to reach out to me at scallesen20@gmail.com if you'd like to connect and/or collaborate!
