Frasassi Grotte

The objective of this project was to create a landscape scene in Unreal Engine, making use of sculting tools, painting tools, materials & textures, and water & wave boxes.

The inspiration for creating this landscape was the Frasassi Grotte that can be found in Ancona, Italy. A beautiful mountain range conceals the entrance to the caves, which open up to winding pockets of stalagmites and pools of water.

Role:

Designer & Developer (Engine: Unreal Engine 5.4)

Media:

~The first 4 images depict the environment that was sculpted in the landscape mode without the addition of any extra components or materials.
~The 5th image depicts the body of water created for the scene, including the color changing component that occurs when the player goes under the water.
